Configurable fields for advanced biopharma partnering needs.

Learn more

Problem Addressed

Most of our customers have their own way of working and managing information related to a partnering opportunity. This information is specific to their needs and internal working processes.​ The solution offered was standard and does not completely cater to the needs of every customer related to capturing critical information related to their opportunity. Configurable fields feature aims to solve this problem.

Feature Overview

Configurable fields will allow users to:

  • Select relevant fields for their needs from an extensive library (100+) of fields for their needs
  • Customize the display name and tooltip of the field to align with internal nomenclature
  • Search, filter and report biopharma partnering opporunities based on configurable fields

Key Aspects

  • Align product with needs of top pharmaceutical companies
  • Feature critical for migrating customers from legacy platform
